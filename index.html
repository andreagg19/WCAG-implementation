About Walter's Health Fund

The Health Fund for Walter White was established by friends and family to help cover the significant expenses of Walter's lung cancer treatment. As a beloved high school chemistry teacher, Walter has inspired many, and now he needs our help.

This fund supports Walter's journey by covering medical expenses, treatment costs, and providing financial security for his family.
